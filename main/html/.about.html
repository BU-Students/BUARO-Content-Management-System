ABOUT BU Alumni Relations Office


The Bicol University Alumni Relations Office serves as the line between the alumni and the BU Community. It seeks to:
  1. Invite wide alumni participation and contribution to the development of the esucation gaols of Bicol University.
  2. Recognize the meritorious services and achivemens of the alumni, and
  3. Provide leadership in coordinating, planning, implementing and monitoring of the development programs of the alumni associations.
  4. Plan and implement Alumni Continuing Education and other activities designed for the continuing professional growth of the Alumni.
  5. Coordinate the organization of an alumni fund and other source of support funds.


Objectives:

To maintain and strengthen a productive partnership and to encourage and facilitate professional and social linkage between Bicol University and its Alumni.



ARO Services for Alumni:
  1. Develop and maintain an Alumni Database of Alumni Records
  2. Recognize Alumni achievements
  3. Collects and Disseminates News/Information
  4. Assits the Unit/College Alumni Associations in Fund Campaigns, Homecomnings & Reunions, Scholarships, Professional Chairs & Faculty Grants
  5. Networks with Alumni Chapters and Friends
  6. Assists in the Continuing Education of the Alumni
